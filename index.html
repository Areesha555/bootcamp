About University of Gujrat

The University of Gujrat (UOG) is a prestigious public university located in Gujrat, Pakistan. Established in 2004, UOG is committed to imparting quality education to students in a wide range of disciplines. It strives to promote research and innovation, with a mission to contribute to the social, economic, and technological development of Pakistan.

Academic Programs

UOG offers a variety of undergraduate, graduate, and postgraduate programs in fields such as:

  • Computer Science
  • Engineering
  • Business Administration
  • Social Sciences
  • Law
  • Medical and Health Sciences
